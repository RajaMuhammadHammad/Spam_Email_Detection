Project Overview

Welcome to the Email Spam Classifier, a web application designed to simplify your email management. Using advanced machine learning algorithms, our system categorizes your emails into Spam and Ham, saving you time and ensuring a clutter-free inbox.

Key Features

  • Login securely with Google using OAuth 2.0.
  • Fetch emails from Gmail inbox using the Gmail API.
  • You can Classify emails into Spam or Ham with high accuracy by providing email Text.
  • ML model is implement to classify the email in spam and Ham .

Spam Email Checker

"Welcome to the Email Spam Classifier, a web application designed to simplify your email management. Using advanced machine learning algorithms, our system categorizes your emails into Spam and Ham, saving you time and ensuring a clutter-free inbox. To use the web application, simply click the Get Started button on the home page. This will redirect you to the Spam Email Checker. Enter your email text into the provided field, then click on the Check Email button. The result will be displayed: if the email is spam, it will appear in red; if it is not, it will appear in green."

Google Login

"Welcome to the Email Spam Classifier, a web application designed to simplify your email management. Using advanced machine learning algorithms, our system categorizes your emails into Spam and Ham, saving you time and ensuring a clutter-free inbox. To use the web application, simply click the Get Started button on the home page. This will redirect you to the Spam Email Checker. You will have the option to log in using your Google Account by clicking the Login with Google button. Once logged in, the application will access your Gmail account to retrieve your email data. After logging in, enter the email text into the provided field, then click on the Check Email button. The result will be displayed: if the email is spam, it will appear in red; if it is not, it will appear in green."
